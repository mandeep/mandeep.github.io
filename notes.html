Generating Obstacle Colors
August 9, 2024

When designing obstacles for Chill But Not Too Chill Obby, I wanted the color of the obstacles to be pastel colors that varied along the color wheel. I decided to write a small snippet that sorts the obstacles based on their position on the Y axis and then applies a color based on that position.

                math.randomseed(os.time())

local obstacles_folder = game.Workspace.Stage27.Spheres
local obstacles = obstacles_folder:GetChildren()

table.sort(obstacles, function(a,b) return a.Position.Y < b.Position.Y end)

for i, obstacle in obstacles do
    obstacle.Color = Color3.fromHSV(i / #obstacles, 0.3, 1)	
end
